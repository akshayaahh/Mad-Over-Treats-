About Us

Mad for Treats began with a simple mission: to offer dogs treats that are as wholesome as they are delicious. What started as a few handmade batches in our home kitchen has grown into a passionate, dog-loving brand trusted by pet parents across India.

All of our treats are:

  • 100% homemade and fresh
  • Free from preservatives, artificial colors, and fillers
  • Crafted in small batches using locally sourced ingredients
  • Approved by our own furry taste testers
